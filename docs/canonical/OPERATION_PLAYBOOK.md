## SECTION 8: AMENDMENT PROCEDURE

### 8.1 Amendment Doctrine

No constitutional-level operational rule may be changed via informal edits, urgent requests, ad hoc decisions, or undocumented consensus. Amendment Procedure is the sole legal path.

### 8.2 Amendment Stages

1. **Proposal Registration**
   - Define target rule(s)
   - Define constitutional reason
   - Define risk envelope and compatibility declaration

2. **Cross-Document Impact Review**
   - MANIFESTO alignment review
   - PROJECT_MASTER_STATE consistency review
   - PROJECT_HANDOFF continuity review
   - SYSTEM_MAP authority review
   - AI_SPECIALISTS communication/governance review

3. **Guardian Constitutional Review**
   - Guardian pass/fail on constitutional integrity

4. **Authority Review Gate**
   - Confirm no authority inversion occurs
   - Confirm zero-authority and safety constraints preserved

5. **Approval & Canonical Publication**
   - Formal acceptance with version increment
   - Canonical publication with full cross-reference update

6. **Post-Amendment Verification**
   - Drift check
   - Contradiction scan
   - Recovery and handoff consistency verification

### 8.3 Amendment Prohibitions

- No retroactive rule mutation without recorded amendment
- No hidden amendments in unrelated documents
- No emergency-only permanent constitutional changes without full procedure
- No partial constitutional rewrites outside cross-document review
