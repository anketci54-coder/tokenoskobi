## Core Rule
Human approval must be bound to immutable evidence context.
Approval can approve or reject a sealed context, but cannot modify evidence.
Any context change requires a new approval.
High-risk approval cannot be single-click.
Fast lane can escalate human review but cannot authorize execution.

## Boundary
V2_35 does not apply runtime code.
V2_35 does not mutate DB.
V2_35 does not call API/RPC.
V2_35 does not enable execution.
V2_35 does not enable wallet access.

## Final Truth
The human approval layer is treated as an attack surface.
The approval screen must show risk delta, poisoning flags, degraded mode, conflict markers, source quorum, and execution lock state.
Human approval cannot bypass execution lock.
